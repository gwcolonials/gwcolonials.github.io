iTalkBB TV

Background

iTalkBB TV was launched as a video streaming platform in July 2019. Its goal is to provide high-quality video resources for audiences interested in Chinese related videos, such as Chinese drama, Chinese movies, and trending videos in the North American Chinese community.

My contribution

I was invited to evaluate and improve the design of its key page, that is the homepage, with the product manager, from the perspective of user experience, in order to enhance increase conversion and retention rates.

Problem

As a website that has only been launched for a year, The overall design of the website could be considered an imperfect MVP. The design had some issues affecting user experience such as consistency and logic errors. The parts that I was working on are marked with flashing red.

Old version of homepage

User research

Due to Corona virus, I chose to use online questionnaire and online interview for the user research. A total of 32 questionnaires were retrieved, one of which was incomplete.

Questionnaire

Analysis

Although native Chinese speaking or native Chinese and English speaking users account for the largest proportion, 4% of users still have difficulty reading the Chinese text content on the page. There is a case that a native Chinese and English speaking user are proficient in Chinese listening but are not familiar with Chinese characters. Therefore, it is necessary to add English descriptions.

78% of users come to the website to kill time, and more than 50% of users already have a target video they need to find when they use the website. Having a clear search box is closer to user needs.

More than half of users chose ‘Neutral’ in the homepage design satisfaction survey, 22% chose ‘Not satisfied’. In comparison with other websites with similar functions, I found that the users care about content attractiveness, function richness, and layout rationality.

Only 3% of testers are registered as members of italkbb tv. The top two factors that affect users' willingness to register are ‘reasonable price’ and ‘personalized customization’. Unfortunately, italkbb tv's free membership registration policy did not bring them a high conversion rate. In the online interview, the tester reported that he was not prompted about membership benefits and free registration functions at all. Therefore, making users aware of member benefits and free registration policy is the key.

In addition to video streaming, 41% of users also spend a lot of time browsing news, and other users are also looking for entertainment and shopping information. In order to capture users, it is necessary to show the functions and content that users expect.

Redesign / login

In the old design, buttons with the same login function have different forms, which caused confusion to users. For example, the login button appears in two shapes in the same modal. In the new design, one of the login buttons form is removed to maintain consistency.

Old version of login

In order to increase the activation rate, users need to be clearly shown the benefits they can enjoy after registration. My idea is to show directly what the exclusive benefits are for signup users, such as watching records, offline video and notification, to increase the attractiveness to unregistered users.

Modified login

When the cursor hovers over the Login button, the Login window pops up and the background fades.

Redesign / lists

An icon is added before the title for easy identification. As shown, a movie projector-style icon before the movie title.

Clickable texts, in this case the movie tags, have been transformed into button-looking to better distinguish them from other texts.

In addition, I found that the confusing logic of buttons brought unnecessary thinking burden to users. In the designer’s imagination, the ‘Other’ tag works as a language related movie tag pointing to a page containing movies in languages other than Chinese and Cantonese, but in fact users often think it refers to movie tags such as action movies or science fiction movies. Moreover, in the a/b test where 'More' tag are eliminated, users consider it works the same as ‘All’ and ‘More’ in functionality.

New section ‘北美都在看’

The results of the user survey show that one of the main reasons for users to browse italkbb is to kill time, and users have a strong demand for news and information. Therefore, the purpose of this section is to take advantage of the resources of the website based in North America, in order to improve the retention rate of users by providing news occurring in North America.

Outcome

The results reported by 21 testers are positive for the improvement of the design. Among them, the user satisfaction rate has increased from an average score of 2.97 in the old version to an average score of 4.25 in the new design. This means that satisfaction has increased by 43%.

100% of users are aware that membership registration is free and the members enjoy benefits. 62% of users are willing to register as a member, which is a big improvement over the 3% membership registration rate in the old design.
