Hotelshare

Introductions

Whether itâ€™s a short business trip or an extended solitary adventure, more travelers are traveling solo than ever before. Although some people find it convenient to travel alone, it is undeniable that it also has disadvantages, such as loneliness and the high spending on accommodations. Hotelshare is a platform that lets people rent out extra beds of their hotel rooms to guests. Unlike Airbnb, Hotelshare not only specializes in sharing hotel rooms but also helps solo travelers to find travel partners.

Key Features

  • Mobile-optimized interface for smartphone users
  • Improved search function, gender preference option added
  • Hosts profile and expectations to be listed along with the hotel information
  • User-friendly and concise booking process
