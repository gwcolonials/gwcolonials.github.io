Trade126

Introductions

The app is a mobile marketplace designed exclusively for college students. Sellers can list their items in less than a minute, while for shoppers finding the perfect product takes just a few clicks. In addition, the app can sort and recommend products based on users from the same school.

Key Features

  • The sub-views categorize items according to student needs.
  • The price is marked in a prominent position, and the level is higher than other information of the product, given that the target user is price sensitive.
  • Gestures are added, such as swiping to delete items.
