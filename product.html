Digging into my education history, you will find that I am a rational person with a Bachelor’s degree in information science and a master’s degree in computer science, while I am also very creative. I have participated in a series of design-related volunteer activities and projects, such as creative advertising design for Coca-Cola. During the HCI intern at George Washington University and the UX course at the University of Michigan, I involved in more UX projects.

Design is not only an aesthetic creation but also a problem-solving activity. As a designer, I’m keen to build high-quality innovative projects that improve overall user experience. Furthermore, I pay attention to creating solutions to growth-related problems, from the perspective of a growth hacking-minded designer. I dream of becoming an expert in the product design field and designing products that have a positive impact on people, the environment and society.
