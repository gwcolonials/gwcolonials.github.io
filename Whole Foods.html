Whole Foods Market Mobile App

Background

Whole Foods Market is an American supermarket chain that offers high-quality products and prepared food. However, as a fast-growing supermarket group, compared to the supermarket chains such as Walmart, Target, and Costco, Whole Foods Market is far left behind in building the mobile apps which have played an increasingly important role in improving the shopping experience. The goal of this project is to evaluate and improve the mobile app of Whole Foods Market to enhance the shopping experience.

Problem

Although Whole Foods Market has an app for customers, it has only three basic sections, namely coupon information, the Prime member QR code, and member portal.

  • All offers in the app, including prime member deals and sale deals, are listed on the same page under the same category. With no search bar on the page, the user needs to browse one by one to find desired items.
  • The app only displays items that are being promoted, not those that are not on the promotion list. Also, product availability is not shown, that means the user would not know whether the item is in stock in the selected store.
  • Prime member QR code cannot be added to the Apple Wallet. The user needs to open the app and pull up the QR code whenever going to checkout in store.

Redesign

1. The home page has been redesigned where contents have been more accurately categorized.

Taking advantage of horizontal scrolling, not only ‘prime & sale’ is being divided into two parts, but it is guaranteed to contain more contents in a limited space. Besides, the search function has been added, and the user will be able to search for products more easily.

2. ‘Prepared Foods’ section as a new feature added to the app would enhance the shopping experience.

Before ordering their food, the user can see quantities of products currently in processing in order to estimate the waiting time. When ordering, the user has the option to checkout in the app.

The app supports adding the QR code to Apple Wallet by which the user can scan the QR code from Apple Wallet at checkout without having to open the app.
